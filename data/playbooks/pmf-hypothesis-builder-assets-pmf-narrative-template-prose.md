## Usage Notes

**When to use this template**:
- Investor presentations and pitch decks
- Team alignment and strategic planning sessions
- Executive reviews and board meetings
- Storytelling where emotional resonance matters

**When to use structured template instead**:
- Internal hypothesis building and rapid iteration
- Quick validation cycles with frequent updates
- Analytical deep-dives where bullet points are clearer

**Tips for compelling narratives**:
1. Make the problem visceral (use emotional language, paint scenarios)
2. Use benefits language, not features (outcome, not capability)
3. Weave metrics throughout (don't relegate to separate "data" section)
4. Show flywheels and compounding effects (how things get stronger over time)
5. Create clear persona divides (Time Masters vs Time Slaves)
6. Connect dimensions (show how value prop reinforces growth, how growth builds moat)
